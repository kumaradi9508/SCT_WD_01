Biography

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, born on 7 July 1981 in Ranchi, Bihar (now Jharkhand), is a former Indian cricketer and one of the most successful captains in the history of Indian cricket. Known for his calm demeanor, sharp mind, and explosive batting skills, Dhoni led India to victories in the ICC T20 World Cup (2007), ICC Cricket World Cup (2011), and ICC Champions Trophy (2013).

Dhoni is also the only captain in the history of cricket to win all three major ICC trophies. He was the captain of Chennai Super Kings (CSK) in the IPL and won multiple titles with them. He is a wicketkeeper-batsman and has a large fan base due to his humble nature, helicopter shot, and leadership qualities.
